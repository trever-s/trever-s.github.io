Hi. I’m Trever.

I lead, teach, and champion design research across organizations like The Library of Congress, Microsoft, Ford Motors, and Charles Schwab.

Recently, my family and I made a radical lifestyle change: we left Seattle and built a new home in the San Juan Islands of Washington State.

Stepping away from my role at the world’s largest design agency, Fjord, I'm excited to find new opportunities to grow my practice and contribute to a remote organization that has a healthy respect for customer centered design.

Previous to Fjord, I worked at NASA, early stage startups. Currently I maintain a few pet projects around compost, mushroom foraging, and hiking with my kids.
