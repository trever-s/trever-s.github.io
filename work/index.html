Hi. I’m Trever.

I'm a user experience researcher with a strength in exploratory inquiry.

My career began as an anthropology-major-turned-developer, with a brief stint at NASA. I became an advocate for user-centered design while folding Web Standards into my code. I honed my UX chops as an entreprenuer, and built upon this passion by leading UX and research efforts at service design firm, Fjord.

My mission is to make research inclusive, reimagine how it's practiced remotely, and make insights actionable for time-strapped teams.

Offline, you'll find me chasing my energetic boys around the San Juan Islands. And although I usually come home empty handed, I'm a member of several mycological societies and enjoy foraging, cooking, and growing mushrooms.
