Challenge

Marketing and sales staff were trained in Marketo, a Marketing Automation tool, but lacked email and landing page templates to perform their work.

Process

The client requested a large catalogue of alternative email and landing page designs. We researched their vertical, as well as specific business needs and presented a lo-fi pdf outling 14 different versions of each asset.

After fielding concerns with a few rounds of refinements as well as walkthroughs we gained buy-in from the staff and major stakeholders.

Hiccups

Presenting the lo-fi wireframes didn't go quite as smoothly as we'd planned. The client was confused as to their purpose. We were able to rectify the situation by explaining the purpose behind each section of the wireframe and what user or business need it resolves.

Results

The templates were received with open arms and have been heavily used since their development in Marketo. The internal team at ABM reported a significant increase in productivity, around 50%, because of the streamlined marketing material. Campain success is up 35% since the first two quarters of implementation.

Continual Improvement

We developed a series of A/B test templates which an alternate call-to-action text and color.
